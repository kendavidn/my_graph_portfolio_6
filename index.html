About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an eight‑week, hands‑on programme where you’ll learn to automate data analysis, content creation, and routine tasks using modern AI tools — without writing code. Highlights include:

  • Automating documents, spreadsheets & data workflows
  • AI‑powered literature reviews & knowledge management
  • Building custom chatbots & retrieval‑augmented systems
  • Creating visuals & presentations with generative models
  • A capstone project to showcase your applied skills
